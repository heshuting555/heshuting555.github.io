Short Biography


Shuting He is currently a final year Ph.D. student at Zhejiang University (ZJU) in Hangzhou, China. She was a visiting Ph.D. student at Computer Vision Lab of ETH Zürich in Switzerland from 2021 to 2022 and a research intern at Alibaba DAMO Academy from 2020 to 2021. She received the B.E. degree from Ximen University (XMU), Xiamen, China, in 2018. Her research interests include machine learning and computer vision, with special emphasis on low-shot learning, person re-identification, and segmentation.
