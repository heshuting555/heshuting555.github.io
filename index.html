Shuting He

Tenure-track Assistant Professor

Shanghai University of Finance and Economics (SUFE)

Email: shuting.he[AT]sufe.edu.cn

Shuting He is currently a tenure-track Assistant Professor at Shanghai University of Finance and Economics (SUFE). Prior to that, she was a Research Fellow at Nanyang Technological University (NTU) in Singapore. She received the Ph.D. degree from Zhejiang University (ZJU), Hangzhou China, in 2023, and the B.E. degree from Xiamen University (XMU), Xiamen China, in 2018. Her research interests include machine learning and computer vision, with special emphasis on embodied intelligence, multimodal large models, intelligent agents, 3D spatial intelligence, and scene generation. For more detail, you can visit our SIGMA Lab website: https://sigma-sufe.com/.

She serves/served as an Area Chair for ICLR 2026, NeurIPS2026, CVPR 2026 and BMVC 2025.
